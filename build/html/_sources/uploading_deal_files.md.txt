# Uploading deal files

This procedure describes how to upload deal files for a BBO tournament, for example, the Children in Need national duplicate event.

1. Ensure that you have the file loaded onto your computer. It must be in the LIN format; not PBN.

1. Log into BBO with the  club's director's username (vebu2111328)

1. Click on the *Account* tab on the right hand side

1. Click on *Deal archive* in the top menu

    <img src="_images/deal_archive.png" width="300">


1. Click on the *+* against *SELECT FOLDER*. A new folder named *Untitled folder* will be created

1. Click on the pencil icon next to this folder to edit the details

1. Change *Untitled folder* to a meaningful name

1. Click on the *Import LIN file* button at the bottom of the panel

1. Click on *choose file* in the dialog box

    <img src="_images/choose_lin_file.png" width="300">

1. Click on the *Import* button. You will see a message confirming that the file has been uploaded

1. Click on the *Director* tab on the right hand side

1. Click on the *Pending Tournaments* tab and the tournament in question. Choose *edit tournament* from the drop down menu.

1. In the *Format* tab, go to *Deal source* and click on *Use random deals* and select *Use saved deals*

1. Click on the *Select folder* button and choose the  folder that you created above

1.  Click on *Select* in the top right hand corner

    <img src="_images/select_folder.png" width="300">

2. Click on modify tournament to save the changes

The deal is now set for the tournament.
