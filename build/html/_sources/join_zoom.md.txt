### Join a Zoom Meeting

You are able to join a Zoom meeting from your browser and so you do not need to download any
special software.

###### Accepting the invitation

You will recieve an invitation similar to that shown below.

![invitation](/images/invitation.jpg)

When you are ready, click on the Zoom meeting url (shown with an arrow).

This will open a new tab in your browser

![browser](/images/browser.jpg)

When you are ready, click on *Join from your Browser* (shown with an arrow).

![join01](/images/join01.jpg)

Enter your name (a nick-name that people know you by will do), click the
 *I am not a robot* box

![join02](/images/join02.jpg)

Click on *Join*.

This will take you to the waiting room.

![waiting](/images/waiting.jpg)

Wait there until the host admits you. They know you are waiting.

When the host admits you, you will see a screen similar to the one below

![joined](/images/joined.jpg)

At this point you might see a button to turn on your microphone.
Click on *Join Audio by Computer*.


![join_audio](/images/join_audio.png)

If not, don't worry, you can turn it on later.

###### In the meeting

As it stands no other participants can see or hear you. You will need to *Start Video* and
*Join Audio*.

 To do this you will need to activate the control bar. You do this my moving the
 mouse pointer over the meeting screen and it will pop up

![control](/images/control.jpg)

The *Start Video* and *Join Audio* buttons are at the bottom left.

If you need to see the control bar later, just move the mouse pointer over the screen.

During the meeting you will see the other participants. Depending on the settings, the
current speaker will be fore-grounded.

At the end of the meeting, the host will close it down and you can shut the browser tab.
