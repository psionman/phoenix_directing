    Transferring $BBO¶

    Buying $BBO for the club¶

    You can use a credit card but it is more straightforward to use PayPal as you do not need to enter you card details again.

    With credit card¶

    If you are using a credit card to buy the $BBO, enter the club’s user name and password. Click on Continue and enter your card details to purchase the $BBO.

    With PayPal¶

    If you are using Paypal to buy $BBO, then, when prompted for the BBO Username and password, enter the club’s BBO login details and proceed as normal.

    Transfer $BBO to a user¶

    Logon to BBO (any BBO id will do) and click on the BB$ button

    ¶

    Click on Transfer BB$

    ¶

    Complete the form - you might have to enter the club’s BBO details
